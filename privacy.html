Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-27

What the app does

Eternal Flux is a personal emotion reflection app. It analyzes facial motion patterns while you watch video to estimate emotional signals (e.g., engagement, fear) and shows an emotion timeline and insights for self-reflection. The app does not provide medical or diagnostic services.

Camera data

  • The camera and TrueDepth system are used to analyze facial motion patterns during a session.
  • Camera footage is not recorded or stored by the app.

TrueDepth and Face Data

Eternal Flux uses Apple’s TrueDepth camera system and ARKit face tracking only during user-initiated sessions to analyze facial motion.

  • The app processes facial geometry and ARKit blend shape coefficients in real time.
  • The app does not capture or store raw camera images, depth maps, face scans, or biometric identifiers.
  • All face-related processing happens locally on your device (in memory only). Face data is not transmitted, uploaded, stored on servers, or shared with third parties.
  • The app does not perform face recognition, identity verification, or biometric identification.

Data storage

  • Session results may be stored locally on your device.
  • User-imported videos and tags are stored on-device.
  • Face-related data from TrueDepth/ARKit is not stored. Only non-identifiable session summaries may be saved on-device.

Sharing

The app may allow you to export a text report that you choose to share. No data is shared automatically.
