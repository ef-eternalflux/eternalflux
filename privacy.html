Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-27

What the app does

Eternal Flux analyzes your facial expressions while you watch video in order to estimate emotional signals (e.g., engagement, fear). This analysis is used to show you an emotion timeline and related insights.

Camera data

  • The camera is used to analyze emotions during a session.
  • Camera footage is not recorded or stored by the app.

Data storage

  • Session results may be stored locally on your device.
  • User-imported videos and tags are stored on-device.

Sharing

The app may allow you to export a text report that you choose to share. No data is shared automatically.
